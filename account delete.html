# Delete Your MyStoryVerse Account MyStoryVerse allows you to permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. ## How to Delete Your Account You can delete your account directly inside the app: - Open the **MyStoryVerse app** - Go to **Profile → Settings → Delete Account** - Confirm your choice Your account will then be permanently deleted. ## Data That Will Be Deleted - Your account details (email, username, profile) - Your stories, posts, comments - Uploaded media (images, audio, video) ## Data That May Be Retained - Technical logs and backups (retained for up to 30 days) - Legal or compliance data (if required by law) After deletion, your data cannot be recovered. For any questions, contact us at **support@mystoryverse.online**
