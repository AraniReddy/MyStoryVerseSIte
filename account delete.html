How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your MyStoryVerse account and all associated data in two ways:

  • In the app (recommended): Open the MyStoryVerse app → Profile → Settings → Delete Account and follow the confirmation prompts.
  • By email: Send a deletion request to support@mystoryverse.online with the subject line Delete My Account. Include the email address associated with your account.

If you choose the in-app option, deletion begins immediately when you confirm. If you use email, we typically process requests within 3 business days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, we will remove the following:

  • Account details (name, email, username, profile information)
  • Your stories, posts, and comments
  • Uploaded media (images, audio, video)
  • Any user-generated metadata directly tied to your account

Data That May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legitimate purposes or legal requirements:

  • Technical logs and backups (retained up to 30 days for recovery and security)
  • Minimal records required for legal or regulatory compliance (only where legally necessary)
Important: Deletion is permanent. Once your account and associated content are removed, they cannot be restored.

Questions or Help

If you have questions or need help, contact our support team at support@mystoryverse.online. Please include your account email and a short description of your request.
